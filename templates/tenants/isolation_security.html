How Your Data Is Isolated

Row-level tenant isolation

Every record carries a tenant foreign key. All queries are automatically scoped to request.tenant, so one organization can never read or write another organization's rows — even within the shared database schema.

Encryption at rest

Stored data is protected with AES-256 encryption on the underlying storage volumes.

Encryption in transit

All traffic between your browser and the application is secured with TLS 1.2 or higher.

Full audit trail

Create, update, and delete actions are recorded in a tamper-evident audit log scoped to your tenant.
